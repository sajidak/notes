### Remote using A/V Out
	=== > > >    | 2.5mm jack
	    1 2 3    | pins
- 1 = Sleeve
- 2 = Ring
- 3 = Sleeve

	Short 1 & 2 to focus
	Short 1 & 3 to shoot
